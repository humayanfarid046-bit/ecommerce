Refund Policy

Effective date: 07 Apr 2026

1. Return Window

Eligible items can be returned within 7 days of delivery unless otherwise stated on the product page.

2. Eligibility

  • Item must be unused, unwashed, and in original condition
  • Original tags and packaging must be intact
  • Proof of purchase/order ID is required

3. Non-Returnable Items

Innerwear, customized products, and products marked non-returnable are not eligible for return/refund unless damaged or incorrect.

4. Refund Timeline

  • Refund is initiated after quality check approval
  • UPI/Wallet/Card refunds may take 3-7 business days
  • Bank settlement timelines depend on payment partner and bank

5. Failed / Duplicate Payments

Failed or duplicate transactions are usually auto-reversed by payment gateway/bank as per standard banking timelines.
